Core Ministries

01

Unshakeable Initiative

A comprehensive two-year discipleship journey built on 12 key elements across three strategic lanes: Unshakeable Lives, Unshakeable Communities, and Unshakeable Impact. This initiative transforms individuals into fully devoted followers of Christ.

02

Buddies Special Needs Ministry

Expanding across all campuses to equip volunteers and serve kids and adults with special needs. Partnering with the Tim Tebow Foundation to host Night to Shine in 2026, celebrating individuals with special needs.

03

Family Discipleship

Launching targeted Discipleship Retreats like "Dad Camp" to strengthen marriages and families. Introducing Kids Summer Camp as a catalyst to reach and disciple Gen Alpha children in their formative years.

04

Growth Track

A three-week journey meeting weekly at each campus where participants discover 12Stone's story, explore their unique spiritual gifts and personality, and learn how to make an impact through meaningful involvement.

05

John C. Maxwell Leadership Center

Inspiring and developing next generation ministry leaders through world-class leadership training and development programs, equipping leaders to multiply their impact in the church and community.

06
