## 1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Business Model and Value Proposition

**Core Mission:** Bridge the gap between humans and machines, transforming maintenance and reliability into a streamlined, cost-efficient advantage while eliminating industrial downtime worldwide.

**Value Proposition:** TRACTIAN provides the industry's first truly integrated Industrial Copilot, combining:
- Proprietary IoT hardware sensors (Smart Trac Ultra)
- AI-powered CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management System)
- Real-time production monitoring (OEE tracking)
- Predictive analytics and prescriptive maintenance guidance

The platform operates as a "single pane of glass" for managing maintenance, monitoring machines, and optimizing production processes—addressing the critical challenge of unplanned downtime that costs manufacturers billions annually.
